Sewage Management - NAGAM

Nagam is responsible for maintaining and operating the sewage systems, ensuring proper waste water disposal, and preventing water contamination.

The department is focused on effective sewage management through regular maintenance of sewage lines, treatment plants, and handling emergency blockages...

The Sewage Management department ensures that sewage lines are regularly cleaned, maintained, and repaired to prevent blockages and ensure smooth flow of wastewater throughout the city.

In addition to maintaining the sewage lines, Nagam also operates sewage treatment plants that treat wastewater before being released back into the environment, ensuring that it meets environmental safety standards.

The department works on handling emergency blockages and leakages that occur in the sewage system, minimizing the risk of contamination and maintaining sanitation in the city.

Furthermore, Nagam promotes awareness programs for citizens, encouraging them to dispose of waste responsibly and avoid practices that lead to clogging the sewage systems.
