Garbage Management and Collection - NAGAM

Nagam is committed to maintaining cleanliness in the city through efficient garbage collection, segregation, and disposal methods to keep the environment clean and healthy.

The department oversees waste management, ensuring that garbage is regularly collected, sorted for recycling, and disposed of safely...

The Garbage Management department is responsible for the efficient collection of household and commercial waste across the city. Garbage is collected on a scheduled basis, and the team works hard to maintain clean streets and public spaces.

In addition to regular garbage collection, Nagar Nigam focuses on waste segregation for recycling purposes. Citizens are encouraged to separate dry and wet waste to aid in recycling, contributing to reducing landfill pressure.

The department also promotes the use of composting for organic waste, further contributing to sustainability efforts. Timely collection and safe disposal of waste are crucial for maintaining a hygienic environment in the city.

By investing in new technologies and infrastructure, Nagam aims to improve waste collection efficiency, reduce waste generation, and promote sustainable waste management practices across the city.
